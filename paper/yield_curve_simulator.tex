\section{Introduction}
Yield curve dynamics drive risk and PnL across fixed income portfolios.
This paper presents a scenario-based framework to analyze curve shifts.

\section{Methodology}
We model parallel, steepening, flattening, and butterfly shocks calibrated
using historical yield movements.

\section{Risk Metrics}
We compute duration, DV01, and convexity to approximate first- and second-order
PnL impacts.

\section{PnL Attribution}
PnL is decomposed into level and curvature effects:
\[
\Delta P \approx DV01 \cdot \Delta y + \frac{1}{2} Convexity \cdot (\Delta y)^2
\]
